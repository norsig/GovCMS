Adds a shortcode and widget to embed Romulus CRM contact forms on any Wordpress Page or Post.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/plugin-name` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
1. Add shortcodes where ever you want citizens to submit requests into Romulus CRM. We recommend at least having a "Contact Us" page.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What if I need help? =

Our team is always happy to help you install this plugin or any other integration. Send us an email at support@romuluscrm.com!

== Screenshots ==

1. Copy your Public Form URL from the department settings section of your Romulus account. You can find this at [https://app.romuluscrm.com/preferences/department/profile](https://app.romuluscrm.com/preferences/department/profile)
2. Add the `romulus_contact_form` shortcode anywhere you want your public form to appear
3. Preview the page or post to see your public form!
